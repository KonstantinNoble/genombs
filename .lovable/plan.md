
# Homepage-Optimierung: Fokus auf Startup-Gründer und Solo-Unternehmer

## Analyse des aktuellen Zustands

Die Homepage verwendet aktuell generische Formulierungen wie:
- "One Question. Six AI Perspectives."
- "Any business decision you need to document"
- "Founders, investors, and regulated teams"

Diese breite Positionierung spricht niemanden direkt an. Die Texte sind technisch korrekt, aber emotional nicht ansprechend für die eigentliche Zielgruppe.

---

## Neue Positionierung: "Dein fehlendes Advisory Board"

Die Kernidee: Startup-Gründer und Solo-Unternehmer haben oft niemanden, mit dem sie wichtige Entscheidungen besprechen können. Synoptas wird zum virtuellen Sparringspartner, der mehrere Perspektiven liefert, bevor man sich festlegt.

---

## Detaillierte Textanpassungen nach Komponente

**Aktuell:**

- Headline: "One Question. Six AI Perspectives."
- Subheadline: "Select 3 AI models. Weight them by what matters to you..."

**Neu:**

- Subheadline: "No co-founder to challenge your thinking? No board to pressure-test your strategy? Get multiple AI perspectives on your next big decision - before you commit."
- Trust-Indikatoren: "Second Opinions" | "Risk Check" | "Decision Records"

### 2. PainPoints.tsx

**Aktuelle Pain Points (zu technisch/AI-fokussiert):**
- "One AI model = one perspective"
- "You can't tell if the answer is reliable"
- "Comparing AI outputs manually is tedious"
- "Your priorities aren't factored in"

**Neue Pain Points (emotionaler, Founder-fokussiert):**
1. **"You're making six-figure decisions alone"**
   - Detail: "Hiring, pivoting, expanding - these decisions shape your company. But without a co-founder or board, you're the only one pressure-testing your logic."

2. **"Mentors give opinions, not structured analysis"**
   - Detail: "Even great advisors have blind spots. And they're not always available when you need to decide this week."

3. **"Gut feeling doesn't scale"**
   - Detail: "Your instincts got you here. But as stakes get higher, 'I felt it was right' won't satisfy investors, partners, or yourself."

4. **"You've googled yourself in circles"**
   - Detail: "Articles, podcasts, Twitter threads - information overload, but no structured way to weigh the options against your specific situation."

**Section Header:**
- Aktuell: "The Problem with Single-AI Answers"
- Neu: "Sound Familiar?"

**Fragen ersetzen/hinzufügen:**

2. **"Can I use this for hiring decisions?"**
   - "Yes. 'Should I hire this expensive senior dev?' is one of the most common questions. Get perspectives on timing, risk, and alternatives."

3. **"Will this replace my advisor?"**
   - "No - it complements them. Advisors give you context and relationships. Synoptas gives you structured multi-perspective analysis you can share with your advisor."

4. **"How is this different from just asking ChatGPT?"**
   - "ChatGPT gives you one answer with no way to verify it. Synoptas shows you where 3 different AI perspectives agree (strong signal) or disagree (dig deeper), weighted by what matters to you."

## Erwartete Ergebnisse

- Startup-Gründer erkennen sich sofort in den Pain Points wieder
- "Fehlendes Advisory Board" trifft einen echten Nerv
- Emotionale Sprache ("decisions alone", "future self will thank you") statt technischer AI-Jargon
- Klare Use-Cases (Hiring, Expansion, Fundraising) statt abstraktem "any decision"
- Solo-Founders fühlen sich direkt angesprochen, Teams sind trotzdem eingeladen
